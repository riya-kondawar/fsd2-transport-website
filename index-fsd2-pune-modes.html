About Pune's Transport

Pune is a bustling city with a variety of transport options to cater to its growing population. From private cars to public transport like buses, metro, and trains, the city offers multiple ways to get around efficiently.

Cars

Cars offer comfort and convenience, especially for families and long-distance travel within the city.

Auto Rickshaws & Taxis

Auto rickshaws are a quick way to navigate through the city's traffic.

Taxis, including Uber, provide a comfortable option for short-distance travel.

PMPML Buses

PMPML buses are the backbone of public transport in Pune.
