About Pune's Transport

Pune is a bustling city with a variety of transport options to cater to its growing population. From private cars to public transport like buses, metro, and trains, the city offers multiple ways to get around efficiently.

Cars

Cars are a popular mode of transport in Pune.
They offer comfort and convenience, especially for families and long-distance travel within the city.

Auto Rickshaws & Taxis

Auto rickshaws and taxis (including services like Uber) are widely used for short-distance travel.
They provide a quick way to navigate through the city's traffic.

PMPML Buses

Pune Mahanagar Parivahan Mahamandal Limited (PMPML) buses are the backbone of public transport in Pune.
They cover extensive routes and are an affordable option for daily commuters.

Metro

Pune Metro is a rapid transit system that provides a fast and efficient way to travel across the city.
It helps reduce traffic congestion and is environmentally friendly.

Trains

Local trains connect Pune to its suburbs and neighboring cities.
They are an essential part of the daily commute for many people working in the city.
